Workhour Record App the web application to save and count your e.g freelance earnings.

How to use?

STEP 1. Add client--> fill the form (hourly rate and additional note are not obligatory)

STEP 2. Add workhour --> fill the form.

STEP 3. Load report--> pick the right report and enjoy the view. Click the button below to download report at pdf format.

Edit client, Delete client--> u know what.

Motivation

Motivation to code that was my previous workplace (i was a tennis coach) where I had to do these reports manually... month by month.. It was annoying :)
